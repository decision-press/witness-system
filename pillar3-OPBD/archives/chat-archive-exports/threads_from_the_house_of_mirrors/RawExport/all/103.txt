### **Suggestions**
Here’s how you could make it read a little smoother and more inviting, without changing your intent:

**1. Streamline repetition and rhythm**  
- “with fresh eyes, renewed energy, and an open invitation to everyone in the parish” → perhaps:  
  &gt; “with fresh eyes, renewed energy, and an open invitation to all.”

**2. Clarify the mission sentence for clarity and impact**  
&gt; “Our goal, and we need your help, is to witness Christ, the Catholic Church, and the Maronite tradition…”  
could become:  
&gt; “Our goal — and we need your help — is to bear witness to Christ through the Catholic and Maronite traditions…”  
That reads more naturally aloud and ties “witness” directly to action.

**3. Sharpen the “five-year plan” line**  
Since you don’t want to highlight the plan itself publicly, maybe soften it:  
&gt; “Our8231 has begun an ambitious journey you can learn more about here.”  
That keeps the intrigue and forward motion without naming specifics.

**4. Strengthen the final CTA (call to action)**  
&gt; “You do not have to be a member of the Knights to provide or receive any services.”  
could become:  
&gt; “Everyone is welcome — you don’t have to be a Knight to take part or to receive support.”  
Friendlier and more active.

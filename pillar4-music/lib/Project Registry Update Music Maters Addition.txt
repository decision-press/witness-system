Witness System — Project Registry Update (v1.0)
Documenting the addition of the fourth official pillar

This document records the expansion of the Witness System from three active pillars to four. The new pillar, Music Matters, has been formally recognized as part of the system’s architecture and stored in memory so it persists across sessions.
The Witness System now contains:
    1. Our8231.org
    2. The God Decision
    3. Open the Pod Bay Door

This update maintains structural integrity and adheres to Purpose Before Process and the Anti-Sloppiness Protocol.

2. Existing Pillars (for context)
1. Our8231.org (8231)
A community-facing project centered on Faith • Family • Community Life.
Currently WordPress 2025, migrating to Astro + Cloudflare Pages.
2. The God Decision (TGD)

3. New Pillar: Music Matters
Definition
Music Matters is now the fourth official project within the Witness System.
It represents the sonic dimension of witness and expression, and will later integrate with hardware (Fantom 8), sequencing experiments, symbolic composition, and potential public-facing content.
Purpose
To explore sound as a mode of witness, creativity, and system expression without collapsing into the other three pillars.
Its job is music, not theology, not community logistics, not meta-architecture.

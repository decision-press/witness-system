---

# **1. Affordance = a thing’s shape tells you how to use it.**

A **door handle** affords pulling.
A **flat plate** affords pushing.
A **scroll wheel** affords scrolling.
A **piano key** affords pressing.

The shape *invites* the action.

You don’t think about it.
Your brain just goes, “Oh, I know how this works.”

That’s an affordance.
